Frequently Asked Questions

Is cannabis legal in Rhode Island?

Yes. Rhode Island legalized recreational cannabis in 2022 via the Rhode Island Cannabis Act. Adult-use sales began in December 2022. Adults 21+ can possess up to one ounce.

How big is Rhode Island's cannabis market?

Rhode Island is a small state with about 1.1 million residents, but benefits from tourism and proximity to Massachusetts and Connecticut cannabis markets. The market is growing steadily.

What license types does Rhode Island offer?

Rhode Island offers cultivator, manufacturer, retailer, and compassion center licenses. The state includes hybrid licenses for existing medical operators and new adult-use licenses with social equity provisions.
