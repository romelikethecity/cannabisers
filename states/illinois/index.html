Frequently Asked Questions

Is cannabis legal in Illinois?

Yes. Illinois legalized recreational cannabis in 2019, becoming the first state to do so through legislative action rather than a ballot measure. Sales began January 2020. Adults 21+ can purchase up to 30g flower, 5g concentrate, or 500mg THC edibles.

How competitive is Illinois' cannabis market?

Very competitive. Illinois generates over $1.5 billion in annual cannabis sales with limited licenses. The state has faced controversy over its social equity licensing program, with delays and legal challenges. Established operators have significant market advantages.

What are Illinois' cannabis tax rates?

Illinois taxes cannabis based on THC content: 10% for flower under 35% THC, 20% for infused products, and 25% for products over 35% THC. Add 6.25% state sales tax plus up to 3% local tax. Effective rates can exceed 35%.
