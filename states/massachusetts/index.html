Frequently Asked Questions

Is cannabis legal in Massachusetts?

Yes. Massachusetts legalized recreational cannabis in 2016 with sales beginning in November 2018. Adults 21+ can purchase up to one ounce of flower or 5 grams of concentrate.

What are host community agreements in Massachusetts?

Massachusetts requires cannabis businesses to negotiate Host Community Agreements (HCAs) with their local municipality. These agreements can include community impact fees of up to 3% of gross sales, creating additional costs and complexity.

How many cannabis licenses does Massachusetts have?

Massachusetts has issued several hundred licenses across cultivation, manufacturing, retail, testing, and transport categories. The Cannabis Control Commission prioritizes social equity and economic empowerment applicants in the licensing process.
