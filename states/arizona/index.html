Frequently Asked Questions

Is cannabis legal in Arizona?

Yes. Arizona legalized recreational cannabis in 2020 via Proposition 207. Adults 21+ can purchase up to one ounce (5g concentrate limit). Medical cannabis has been legal since 2010.

How many cannabis licenses does Arizona issue?

Arizona initially capped recreational licenses tied to existing medical dispensaries, with additional social equity licenses being awarded. The state has over 130 licensed dispensaries operating in both medical and recreational markets.

What makes Arizona's cannabis market unique?

Arizona allows dual-use licenses (medical and recreational from the same location), has a large tourist population in Phoenix and Scottsdale, and borders multiple states where cannabis remains illegal, creating strong demand.
