Frequently Asked Questions

Is cannabis legal in Montana?

Yes. Montana legalized recreational cannabis in 2020 via Initiative 190. Adults 21+ can possess up to one ounce. Sales began January 2022.

What are Montana's cannabis regulations?

Montana regulates cannabis through the Department of Revenue. The state requires seed-to-sale tracking, mandatory testing, and compliance with local government opt-in requirements. Counties and municipalities can choose to prohibit cannabis businesses.

How does Montana's geography affect cannabis businesses?

Montana's low population density and vast geography create unique distribution challenges. Transport costs are higher, and many rural areas have opted out of allowing cannabis businesses, concentrating the market in cities like Billings, Missoula, and Great Falls.
