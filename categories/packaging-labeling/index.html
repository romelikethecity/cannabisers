Frequently Asked Questions

What are cannabis packaging requirements?

Most states require child-resistant packaging (ASTM D3475 certified), opaque containers, specific label information (THC content, warnings, batch numbers), and tamper-evident seals. Requirements vary significantly by state and product type.

How do I find compliant cannabis packaging?

Work with suppliers who specialize in cannabis packaging and understand your state's specific requirements. Ask for compliance documentation, ASTM certifications, and references from operators in your state.

What's the typical lead time for custom cannabis packaging?

Stock compliant packaging ships in 3-7 days. Custom printed packaging takes 4-8 weeks for initial orders. Plan 6-8 weeks ahead for new product launches to avoid using generic packaging.
