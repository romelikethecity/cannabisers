Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a dispensary build-out cost?

Budget $100-300 per square foot for a dispensary. A typical 2,000 sqft retail space runs $200,000-600,000 including design, permitting, security infrastructure, display cases, and point-of-sale areas. Premium locations in competitive markets run higher.

How long does a cannabis facility build-out take?

Dispensaries: 3-6 months from permit to opening. Cultivation facilities: 6-12 months for indoor, 3-6 months for greenhouse. Processing labs: 4-8 months. Permitting often adds 2-4 months before construction begins, depending on local government efficiency.

What's unique about cannabis construction?

Specific ventilation requirements for odor control, reinforced vault rooms, surveillance infrastructure, sealed clean rooms for processing, high-capacity electrical for cultivation lighting, specialized flooring for wet environments, and compliance with state-specific facility requirements.
