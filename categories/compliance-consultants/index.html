Frequently Asked Questions

What does a cannabis compliance consultant do?

They help you navigate state licensing applications, develop standard operating procedures, prepare for inspections, manage seed-to-sale tracking, and stay current with regulatory changes. Some specialize in specific license types like cultivation or retail.

When should I hire a compliance consultant?

Before you apply for a license. A good consultant saves months of back-and-forth with regulators and dramatically improves your approval odds. They also help during expansions, license renewals, and when regulations change.

How much do cannabis compliance consultants charge?

Hourly rates range from $150-350. License application packages run $5,000-25,000 depending on state complexity. Ongoing compliance retainers typically cost $1,500-5,000 per month.
